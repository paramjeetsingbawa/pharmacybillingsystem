Tutorial for billing guideline


Search Product for billing

To Search for a product: Type product name (partial or exact), or Batch number or Company name and Press Enter key.
Use arrow keys or tab key to move in selected products
To select and enter quantity of purchase, press enter or click using mouse

Product Amount and Purchase Quantity

Press Arrow or Tab key to start typing. User can change sale date only with mouse.
Amount per unit is calculated based on MRP entered originally while inserting product information
Profit Calculation : Profile is calculated based on purchase rate And Tax information
If user entered amount per unit manually, The System will check for loss, and Give Alert symbol if the product may give loss in invoice.
If the entered amount per unit and quantity is giving, No profit and No loss, then yellow alert will come in screen

Invoice Generation and Save Sales Data


Enter customer name, customer contact, doctor who prescribes for this medications.
You can give additional discount on the final amount.
Remember to check the profit calculation at the bottom of generate invoice pop-up window.
Ctrl+I will save the sales details and update quantities of products sold automatically in database.

Page Shortcuts


Ctrl+F----To Focus the cursor one product name filter.
Ctrl+H----To Focus the selection one header.
Ctrl+P----To preview the selected product items ready for billing.
Ctrl+I/G--To generate the invoice, Enter customer name, mobile, prescriber details etc.
Ctrl+I----To save and print the invoice details in database.
Ctrl+E----To open the edit window of selected product.
F1--------To open page help.
F2-------To get product statics, sale analysis for the selected product.
F4--------To deleted the selected product.
F5--------To update the product quantity to Zero.
F10--------Hide Sale entry
